# The Authentic AI Voice Seeker
## ICA Avatar | AI Stewardship | Richard Schreiber

---

## CANONICAL NAME
The Authentic AI Voice Seeker

## WHO THEY ARE
Professional in any field navigating AI adoption. Christian or faith-adjacent — looking for a voice that shares their values without preaching. Suspicious of AI hype and AI fear in equal measure. Wants someone who has done the actual work and will report honestly.

## THEIR QUESTION
Can I use AI in a way that is ethical, effective, and consistent with who I am — without having to choose between my values and staying competitive?

## WHAT THEY NEED FROM RICHARD
The anti-replacement stance stated plainly. The faith thread — present but not performative. The practitioner standard — demonstrated through specificity. Permission to move toward AI without abandoning what they believe.

## WHAT THEY DO NOT NEED
AI cheerleading that ignores legitimate concerns. AI fear-mongering that ignores legitimate opportunity. Anyone performing certainty they do not have.

## PRIMARY TRACK AND PLATFORMS
Cross-platform avatar. All tracks. Primary platforms: Substack, True Journalism podcast, LinkedIn, RichardSchreiberAI.com.

## HOW AGENTS USE THIS AVATAR
- Stephen / Maya / Isaac: Keep the faith thread underground and load-bearing — never the headline unless the piece is explicitly about faith. Use moral obligation language ("professional responsibility," "what we owe each other") over religious instruction. Maintain the practitioner register: report from inside the work, not above it. Preserve verbatim faith signals when they appear in source material. Close on agency and reality.
- Voice Check: Does this allow them to adopt AI without compromising their conscience? If it forces a choice between values and competence, rewrite it.
